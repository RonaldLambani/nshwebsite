About Us

NSH Youth Empowerment mission is to empower youth (age 18 - 35 years) to enhance health, end hunger, and overcome hardship to care for the communities at large, including taking care of orphaned kids due to HIV/Aids or Cancer impact.

Our Mission

NSH envisions the world in which the most vulnerable people will have the power to lift themselves out of poverty and to create vital, healthy lives for their families and communities now and for the future .

FOCUS

We implement various programmes that focus on the entrepreneur and supplier development , as well as IT and vocational skills development from tender Age of 8years-35years old youth. Our focus areas are closely interlinked and strive toward the outcome of enhanced livelihoods and a thriving commmunity

NSH 4079 children and youth in jhb North most vulnerable and isolated communities from 2013 to date . We are committed on demonstrating corporate social responsibility and engaging in activities that improve the social , environmental and economic well-being of a community as well as offfering , leadership and mentorship.

We build strong communities through Technology & Economic inclusion

Like many townships , lack of access to technology is a glaring problem in these townships, exacerbating already deep inequality. This is many our interventions are focused on.technological training. NSH Skills Studio serves as a township tech hub where we will run a variety of tech programmes for both adults and children. We work in Cosmo City, Itsoseng, Kyasands, Zandspruit, Musawawa, Thabo Mbeki, Honeydew townships in Johannesburg characterised by high unemployment and low social well-being. We aim to connect local entrepreneurs to opportunities to earn sustainable incomes by providing them with the resources, training and access to markets they need to grow their businesses. By placing special emphasis on the upliftment of youth, women and people with disabilities, to ensure that no one gets excluded
